IoT and AI



Today, the Internet of Things (IoT) is driving the development of machine learning systems that can connect devices and collect data in real-time. This data can then be used to automate processes, detect patterns, and make predictions. By leveraging powerful algorithms, IoT machine learning systems can help businesses make smarter decisions, improve efficiency, and gain competitive advantages. This article covers the basics of IoT machine learning and how it works. We will also explore some of the benefits, challenges, and opportunities associated with this technology.
