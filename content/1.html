IoT and AI


The Internet of Things (IoT) is rapidly becoming an essential part of our lives. It has enabled us to connect and control a variety of devices, from home security systems to self-driving cars. By using machine learning, IoT systems can become smarter and more efficient. This article will explore the basics of IoT machine learning, and discuss how it can be used to make our lives easier. We will also look at some of the challenges associated with implementing machine learning into IoT systems. Finally, we will consider the potential for IoT machine learning to revolutionize the way we interact with our environment.
