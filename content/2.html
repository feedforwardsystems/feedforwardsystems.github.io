IoT and AI



IoT systems are becoming increasingly popular in recent years. Machine learning, a type of artificial intelligence, is being used to make these systems smarter and more efficient. IoT systems use data from connected devices to learn from and make better decisions for the user. This technology is being used for all sorts of applications, from home automation, healthcare, and industrial applications, to smart cities and transportation. In this article, we will explore the basics of IoT machine learning, the potential of this technology, and its applications.
